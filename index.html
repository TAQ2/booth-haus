A multimedia exhibition showing and selling artwork from the biggest property guardianship in the UK.
Raising money to resettle a refugee family.

We are passionate about showcasing our creativity. Living in Booth House has been more than a living space, it has been a source of inspiration, development and belief in our respective crafts. This guardianship has given rise to vast opportunities, and this art fair represents the beauty of the diverse talents we want to share with you.

Our events are in support of E123 Welcomes Refugees Campaign. Proceeds are going towards resettling a refugee family in East London, supporting them to be welcomed into our community. Find out more information here.
